ESP Pins:

GPIO (Output Ports):
    GPIO1...16  Analog / Digital

UART1 (MIDI):
    GPIO17  TX
    GPIO18  RX

USB:
    GPIO19  D-
    GPIO20  D+

SD SPI:
    GPIO34  CS
    GPIO35  MOSI
    GPIO36  SCK
    GPIO37  MISO

JTAG: (see for connector info: https://docs.espressif.com/projects/espressif-esp-iot-solution/en/latest/hw-reference/ESP-Prog_guide.html)
    GPIO39  MTCK
    GPIO40  MTDO
    GPIO41  MTDI
    GPIO42  MTMS

UART0 (Programming):
    GPIO43  TXD
    GPIO44  RXD
