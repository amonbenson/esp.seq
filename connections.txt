ESP Pins:

Outputs (variable):
    GPIO1...8   Analog (PWM)
    GPIO9...16  Digital

Status LED (variabe):
    GPIO17      Status LED

USB (fixed):
    GPIO19  D-
    GPIO20  D+

SD SPI (fixed):
    GPIO34  CS
    GPIO35  MOSI
    GPIO36  SCK
    GPIO37  MISO

JTAG (fixed): (see for connector info: https://docs.espressif.com/projects/espressif-esp-iot-solution/en/latest/hw-reference/ESP-Prog_guide.html)
    GPIO39  MTCK
    GPIO40  MTDO
    GPIO41  MTDI
    GPIO42  MTMS





output layout definition:
    uint8 note_count
    uint8 drum_count
    bool velocity_enabled
    bool trigger_enabled
    set_drum(id, value)
    set_note(id, value)
